DRAKE

Drake, born Aubrey Drake Graham, is a Canadian rapper, singer, songwriter, actor, and entrepreneur. He first gained recognition for his role on the teen drama television series "Degrassi: The Next Generation" before pursuing a career in music. Drake's music blends elements of hip-hop, R&B, and pop, and he is known for his catchy melodies, introspective lyrics, and versatility. Throughout his career, Drake has released several critically and commercially successful albums, including "Take Care," "Nothing Was the Same," and "Scorpion." He holds numerous Billboard chart records and has won several awards, including multiple Grammy Awards. He remains one of the most influential and successful artists in contemporary music
