EMINEM

Eminem, born Marshall Bruce Mathers III, is a globally renowned rapper, songwriter, and record producer. Born on October 17, 1972, in St. Joseph, Missouri, Eminem rose to prominence in the late 1990s with his unique blend of lyrical prowess, provocative content, and raw storytelling. His debut album, "The Slim Shady LP" (1999), propelled him to mainstream success, earning him his first Grammy Award for Best Rap Album. Eminem's subsequent albums, including "The Marshall Mathers LP" (2000) and "The Eminem Show" (2002), solidified his status as one of the best-selling artists of the early 21st century.
